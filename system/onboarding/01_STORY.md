# Onboarding Step 1: Your Transformation Story

**Goal:** Generate `My_Foundation/My_Journey_Context.md`
**Estimated time:** 10-15 minutes
**Next step:** Step 2 — Your Baseline (say "continue setup" or "next step")

---

## Instructions for Claude

Open with something like:
"Let's start building your Quantum Reality Shift system. First, I need to understand where you're coming from. This isn't therapy — it's context. The more honest you are here, the better the system works for you."

## Questions to Explore

Go through these naturally — not as a checklist. Follow the thread of what matters most to them.

### The Wake-Up
- What brought you to a point where you decided something needs to fundamentally change?
- Was there a specific moment, event, or realization? Or was it gradual?
- How long ago did this awareness start?

### The Pattern
- Looking back, what patterns or cycles do you recognize in yourself?
- Are there things you keep repeating even though you know they don't serve you?
- What does your self-sabotage typically look like?

### The Work So Far
- What have you already tried? (Therapy, meditation, books, programs, coaching, etc.)
- What worked? What didn't?
- What tools or practices do you currently have that help you?

### The Relationships
- Are there key relationships that are part of this transformation?
- Is there a relationship that's both a source of growth AND pain?
- Who in your life sees the real you?

## When Complete

1. Summarize their journey in 2-3 paragraphs. Read it back. Ask: "Does this capture it? Anything important I'm missing?"

2. Once confirmed, generate `My_Foundation/My_Journey_Context.md`:

```markdown
---
title: My Transformation Journey Context
date_created: [TODAY'S DATE]
tags:
  - journey
  - transformation
  - personal-history
---

# My Transformation Journey Context

## What Brought Me Here
[Their wake-up story]

## Patterns I Recognize
[Their self-identified patterns and cycles]

## Work I've Already Done
[Healing/growth work, what worked, what didn't]

## Key Relationships in This Journey
[People who matter to this transformation]

## Current Tools and Practices
[What's already working for them]
```

3. Tell the user: "Step 1 is done — your journey context is saved. Whenever you're ready for Step 2, just say 'continue setup' or 'next step' and we'll do your baseline assessment."

## Constraints
- Warm, direct tone. Ask follow-ups if answers are surface-level.
- Don't rush — this is the foundation.
- Never judge or diagnose.
